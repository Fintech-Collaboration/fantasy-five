Meet the Team

What is Fantasy-Five?

A prototype for a sandbox that allows users to practice trading cryptocurrencies with no real financial risk. For our first version, we have provided historical OHLCV data on 12 cryptocurrencies, 4 in each of the following categories: High Cap(>$5b), Mid Cap(251m-4.99b), Low Cap (<250m). Along with the historical data, ML models and predictions have been made using multiple classifiers to help assist in navigating through the crypto environment.

When first navigating to the website, users will have to either sign up or sign in. When logged in, they will be able to switch through various tabs containing information. They will also be allowed to make mock portfolios and select which coins they would like to “invest in” and how much.

Pitch

Initially, we will be deploying for free solely trying to grow our user base and community behind/using the project. However, in order to spur advancements and make it easier to provide higher quality data and offerings, we would offer a pre-payment on our premium tier. Meaning, users purchase ‘x’ months/years and prepay it so that we can use those funds to purchase upgraded APIs, hiring developers, and covering other costs that can arise. When the premium version is ready to go live, users who pre-bought would be credited with the appropriate amount of membership.

What's next?

In the future, we would like to expand on the functionality of the website. In terms of the coins available for investment and adding additional trading functions, such as buy/sell limits, automatic transactions, and more.

Potentially publish the sandbox to the blockchain and make it possible for users to get practice with staking and trading with live gas fee rates.

Inspiration

The inspiration behind Fantasy-Five stems from the amount of attention currently being paid to the crypto space. Our team sought to create an environment where people interested in the crypto space, whether experienced or just starting out, can practice buying, selling, and navigating through the rapidly advancing industry. Users have the freedom to explore their crypto curiosity on Fantasy-Five, then apply what they have learned to their real wallets and portfolios.
